histogram :
+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
This graph is the frequency distribution of values. 

hist()

v -> c(5, 7, 8, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, 12)

hist(v, main="",
     xlab="",
     ylab = "",
     xlim= c(min, max),
     ylim= c(min, max),
     col = "red",
     border = "blue",
     breaks = number
     )

main : title of the graph
xlab, ylab : Label for x-axis and y-axis
xlimm ylim : min and max values for x-axis and y-axis
col : color of the bars
border : border of the bars
breaks : break points (a suggestion of number of bins)
